About

Challenge
All people have the very same problem with ordering along with delivery system(which includes :ordered item not available,delays in delivery of the order,no setting a date or time for the order you want,no option to be assisted when you not sure about what you want order) .
Solution
An App that allows users to get assisted via phone call when not sure of what to do via the app,user can schedule when to order and expected delivery time,track their orders,get called when what they ordered is not available so they can change their order,and can pay with your bank cards even with points/gift card intergrated within the app
