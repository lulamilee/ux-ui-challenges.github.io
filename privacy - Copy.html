Mission

Our mission is to share and grow the world's knowledge. The StudyZel application offers a place to ask questions and connect with people who contribute unique insights and quality answers. This empowers people to learn from each other and to better understand the world.

StudyZel is a Question and Answer mobile application for University Students. For the purpose to boost student's knowledge and for students to share that knowledge amongst one another in the platform.
